Ditch your mouse. Surf the web with just your keyboard.

DeadMouse is a Google Chrome extension that will let you click links on web pages just by typing them out.

Try it out right on this page!

Start typing the text of any of the following links. Press tab to cycle through multiple options. And don't touch that mouse!

  • xkcd
  • Google
  • Charlie bit my finger (YouTube)
  • The other most awesome video in the universe (YouTube)
  • Rodent (Wikipedia)

Features

  • Follow links just by typing
  • Fuzzy-matching on link text
  • Tab-cycle through multiple matches
